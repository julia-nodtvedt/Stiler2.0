It'll be All White on the Night: All White Outfit Inspiration

by Mags Yip

Let’s face it. Some people just can’t wear white. If you’re the type of girl/guy who has trouble eating spaghetti at a restaurant or chooses white over red wine purely for the sake of your dry cleaning bill, then white isn’t for you. But for some lucky few out there, for some sophisticated eaters and drinkers who don’t spill things down themselves regularly (seriously who are these people?), white is all right. If you’re one of these white-wearing super humans, here are 3 looks to inspire your AW15 or upcoming SS16 wardrobe.

Winter is Casual

White, like black, is one of those colours that can be anything you want it to be. White is slick. Yet white is pretty. White is bold. Yet white is understated. White is summery. Yet it’s wintery like snow. White is ceremonial but it’s also casual – and blogger Minnie has created the ultimate casual look in white with this gorgeous OOTD.

White Suit

Who said women couldn’t wear suits? This all-white tailored look by Kaitlyn Ham of Modern Legacy Blog is suave and sexy. And it kicks ass because she’s teamed this slick ensemble with a pair of crisp white sneakers.

Winter Whites

It’s not exactly tropical out there and that’s why Stephi LaReine’s outfit is such a smart take on a summery colour. White is certainly a summer favourite but this look is teamed with black tights and some chunky ankle boots for a bit more winter oomph! We’re so in love with this OOTD and we think it’s so transitional too.
